Promethea Station

24th IAA Humans in Space Symposium

A presentation of Promethea Station at the 24th IAA Humans in Space Symposium — a modular orbital platform in low Earth orbit designed to validate the two technologies most critical to long-duration human deep-space travel: an artificial Martian gravity, and a controlled ecological life support system (CELSS).

The presentation covers the station's structural design, the CELSS inflatable greenhouse modules, the artificial gravity parameters derived from SpinCalc — developed as part of the HB2 Orbital Shift studio at TU Wien.
