IAC 2026 — Abstract Submission

Abstract submitted to the International Astronautical Congress 2026, developing a methodology for personalised biological life support systems in the context of long-duration human spaceflight and Mars surface habitation.

The abstract addresses the intersection of habitat design, closed ecological systems, and individual-level biological parameter variation — contributing to the broader research programme on autonomous life support sizing at TU Wien.
