About

Luka Pejic

Hi, my name is Luka!

I'm currently a 3rd-year Master's student at the Technical University of Vienna.

My design approach is based on how evidence-based reasoning and architectural thinking can merge into technically grounded and human-centred concepts. Research, data, or necessity back every design decision in my projects.

Outside of work, I'm interested in mathematics, physics, biology, chemistry, and human-factor- and long-term design strategies — subjects that help bridge disciplines and turn theoretical into practical.
